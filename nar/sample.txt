Acme Corp Employee Handbook — Remote Work Policy

Eligibility: All full-time employees who have completed their 90-day onboarding
period are eligible to work remotely up to three days per week. Contractors and
interns must obtain written approval from their reporting manager before working
remotely.

Equipment: The company provides a laptop, an external monitor, and a stipend of
$300 per year toward home-office equipment. Employees are responsible for
maintaining a reliable internet connection of at least 50 Mbps.

Core Hours: Regardless of location, all employees must be available during the
core collaboration hours of 10:00 AM to 3:00 PM in their local time zone.
Meetings should be scheduled within this window whenever possible.

Security: Remote workers must use the company VPN when accessing internal
systems and must enable full-disk encryption on all devices. Public Wi-Fi may
only be used in combination with the VPN.

Expense Reimbursement: Co-working space memberships are reimbursable up to $200
per month with prior manager approval. Receipts must be submitted within 30 days
of the expense through the Concur portal.
